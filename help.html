Scratch off Customization Guide



Customization for your personalized scratch-off card is done using the customization panel. This panel can be hidden to preview the changes. Let’s first take a look at the elements of the panel before going through the instructions. Then, you can create the link for your personalized scratch-off card in three easy steps. Please note: The customization page does not have a save feature. To avoid losing your progress, complete your personalization in one session. Leaving the browser may erase your changes from the cache


The panel's top bar can be used to hide the panel when clicked or touched.

Next is the Button for Help page. This is the button to show the help page. You can use "back" button of your browser to return to the customization page.

Button to hide the panel. This is the button to collapse the panel. There are 3 ways to hide the panel in order to see the page for changes you made. 1) click or touch to the panel's top bar, 2) click "Hide the Panel" button, 3) click or touch anywhere outside the panel. (note: you can't completely hide the panel. for that you can always go to finish tab, create the link and go to link to see the real page.)


Panel's tabs for different parameters. There are 3 tabs to make changed on the page. "Step 1" tab is used for entering the custom title , the text under title and the message under the scratch area. The background image, font type and size of the title, shape of the scratch area and scratch area's foreground image can be changed on this tab. "Step 2" tab is used for effects like confetti and sound. "Finish" tab is used for generating your final link to the personalized scratcher to share.

Instructions:

Step-1:

1) First select your background image from the dropdown.

2) Then enter your title. Title is the text that appears at the top with larger font size. It has 25 characters limit.

3) Choose the font for your title from the dropdown.

4) Choose the relative font size for your title from the dropdown. You can select "Smaller," "Normal," or "Bigger." However, use this option with caution. While the page is designed to be responsive and work on most common screen sizes, larger fonts may cause certain elements of the scratcher to go off-screen or overlap with other elements on smaller devices.

5) Enter the text under your title. This text has 50 character limit.

6) Enter the text to be hidden under the scratch area. This text has 110 characters limit.

7) Select the shape of the scratch area. Make sure this selection aligns with the foreground you will choose next, as some foregrounds work better with specific shapes. For example, "Gift Package" foregrounds pair better with a square scratch area, while the "Halloween1" foreground is better suited for a circular scratch area.

8) Select the foreground image for the scratch area. This image will cover the text beneath it on the scratch area.


Step-2:

1) Check/uncheck whether you want confetti effect after the scratch area is scratched. Scratch to see the effect.

2) Check/uncheck whether you want sound effect after the scratch area is scratched. Scratch to see the effect.


Finish:

1) Choose whether you want the personalized link to be in the form of a short URL. Since the personalization is embedded in the link, the generated URL will exceed 245 characters, which may look unappealing when shared. If you select this option, the link will be shortened, making it easier to share.

2) Enter the text that will appear before the link when sharing. See the next image for an example. This text has a 25-character limit.

3) Click the green button to generate your personalized link.


From the message box that pops up, it is recommended to first click "Go to the Link" to test the page and preview how it looks. Once you're satisfied, you can click the "Share" button to share your personalized scratcher via your preferred messaging app or social media platform. (Note: The red text in the message popup box represents the shortened link in text format, which can be copied manually if needed. It will not appear unless the "Shorten URL" option is selected.)


The image above shows an example of the link shared on WhatsApp after pressing the share button in the message popup. The original personalized link is long and contains all the customization details. If it doesn’t look appealing, you can use the "Shorten URL" option. The "Here is something for you" text can be changed under the "Finish" tab, as explained in the previous section above.

Important Notes/Troubleshooting:

1) This product requires an internet connection to customize and use. Recipients must have internet access and a JavaScript-enabled browser to view the webpage and scratch the card.

2) This product supports smartphones, tablets, desktops, and laptops with an internet browser. It is designed to work on most modern devices; however, due to device variability, compatibility cannot be guaranteed on every device.

3) You must only use latest versions of Safari, Chrome, Edge, Opera or Firefox browser. IE or other browsers aren't supported.

4) Although it supports landscape mode, it is recommended to use it in Portrait mode on mobile devices.

5) If you observe too large text and objects on Firefox/Mozilla browser, accessibility option might have been turned on. Disable it as seen in the image below to see the scratcher in original size.


6) On Safari, if you see a blank page (Safari isn't loading websites or quits on your iPhone, iPad), please visit https://support.apple.com/en-us/102456 to troubleshoot. Among those most likely reason is wireless network. Try disabling the wireless network and switching to cellular network.

Error/Warning Messages:

1) "The text for the 'title' is too big to fit to the screen. So please either choose different font or smaller font size. Make sure you pick words shorter than 17 characters." : This error occurs when: a) The selected font or font size for the title is too large to fit on the screen. b) The title text is too long due to very long words or missing spaces between words and/or after punctuation marks. To fix this, you can, select a different font, reduce the font size, ensure there are spaces between words and after punctuation and use shorter words (under 17 characters). See the examples below: In the first example, missing spaces between words cause the title to overflow. After adding spaces, the problem is fixed.



2) "Your message under scratch area contains words that are too long to fit in. Please use shorter words or make sure you put space after punctuations. Please correct the error to continue." : This message appears when your text includes words that are too long for the scratch area, causing it to overflow. If not fixed, part of your message may not be visible. To resolve this, a) Use shorter words, preferably less than 17 characters. b) Make sure there’s a space after punctuation or between words. See the example below. Arrows indicate where a space is missing after punctuation or between two words.



3) "The font size has been reduced automatically because the text boxes were going out of the screen or overlapping" : This is a warning message. If the font size is too large for the title—especially with certain fonts that appear bigger even at the same point size—the title text may extend beyond the screen or overlap with other elements. To prevent this, the web page performs an automatic check and adjusts the font size as needed to ensure all text remains visible on screen. The title font size is gradually reduced automatically. If the issue cannot be resolved through these adjustments, the error described in (1) above will be displayed. As a result of this adjustment, your title text may appear smaller than the size you originally selected. If you prefer to avoid this, try choosing a different font.

4) "Unfortunately sharing is not supported by your browser/platform. Please go to the link and use your browser's address bar to copy the link instead" : This error is displayed if sharing function is not supported by your browser or platform. You can first try using a different browser. If you still get this error, you can instead copy the link yourself and share it in your messaging app of choice. In order to copy the link you can either click "Go to the Link" button and copy the link from your browser's address bar or copy the short URL (if you chose this option) displayed in the message box. See the screenshots below.



5) "Server is busy to handle the URL shortening request. Try again a few minutes later." : We use a third-party service to shorten customized links. If you repeatedly request shortened links, create a link shortly after your previous one, or if many users are using the service at the same time, you may encounter this error. Please try again after a few moments. If the error persists, you can cancel the "Shorten URL" option and create your link without shortening it—however, the link will be longer. If you continue to experience this issue after waiting, please contact the shop for assistance.

6) "An error occurred during URL shortening process.Please check your internet connection or uncheck 'Shorten URL' to try without this option." : We use a third-party service to shorten customized links. If your internet connection is unavailable or the third-party service is temporarily down, you may encounter this error. Check your connection and try again in a few moments. Alternatively, you can create your link without using the shortening option. If the problem persists, please contact the shop for further assistance.
