Scratch off Customization Guide



Customization for your personalized scratch off card is done using the customization panel. It is a panel that can be hidden to see the changes. Lets have a look at the elements of this panel before going into the instructions. Then The link for your personalized scratch off card can be created in 3 easy steps.


The panel's top bar can be used to hide the panel when clicked or touched.

Next is the Button for Help page. This is the button to show the help page. You can use "back" button of your browser to return to the customization page.

Button to hide the panel. This is the button to collapse the panel. There are 3 ways to hide the panel in order to see the page for changes you made. 1) click or touch to the panel's top bar, 2) click "Hide the Panel" button, 3) click or touch anywhere outside the panel. (note: you can't completely hide the panel. for that you can always go to finish tab, create the link and go to link to see the real page.)


Panel's tabs for different parameters. There are 3 tabs to make changed on the page. "Step 1" tab is used for entering the custom title , the text under title and the message under the scratch area. The background image, font type and size of the title, shape of the scratch area and scratch area's foreground image can be changed on this tab. "Step 2" tab is used for effects like confetti and sound. "Finish" tab is used for generating your final link to the personalized scratcher to share.

Instructions:

Step-1:

1) First select your background image from the dropdown.

2) Then enter your title. Title is the text that appears at the top with larger font size. It has 25 characters limit.

3) Choose the font for your title from the dropdown.

4) Choose the relative font size for your title from the dropdown. You can choose "Smaller", "Normal" or "Bigger". However, use this option carefully. Although the page is designed as responsive to work on most common screen sizes, the bigger fonts might cause certain elements of scratcher go out of screen or under other elements in some small screen devices.

5) Enter the text under your title. This text has 50 character limit.

6) Enter the text to be hidden under the scratch area. This text has 110 characters limit.

7) Select the shape of the scratch area. Make this selection inline with the foreground you will choose next. Some foregrounds are better with certain shapes. For example, "Gift Package" foregrounds are better with square shape scratch area. "Halloween1" foreground is better with circle shape scratch area.

8) Select the foreground image for the scratch area. This is the image that appears on the scratch area hiding the text under.


Step-2:

1) Check/uncheck whether you want confetti effect after the scratch area is scratched. Scratch to see the effect.

2) Check/uncheck whether you want sound effect after the scratch area is scratched. Scratch to see the effect.


Finish:

1) Choose whether you want the personalized link to be in form of short URL. As the personalization is hidden on the link, the link to be generated will be more than 245 characters which may look bad when shared. If you check this option, the link will be in short form, easier to share.

2) Enter the text that appears before the link while sharing. See the next image for an example. This text has 25 character limit.

3) Click the green button to generate your personalized link.


From the message box that pops up, it is recommended to first click "Go to the Link" to test the page and see how it all looks. Then you can click the "Share" button to share your personalized scratcher to the person(s) through the messaging app or social media of your preference. (note: the red text in the message popup box is the shortened link in text format that can be copied manually if needed. It will not appear if "shorten URL" option is not selected.)

Important Notes/Troubleshooting:

1) This product requires internet to customize it and run. Receivers must have internet connection and a javascript-enabled browser to see the webpage and scratch the card.

2) It supports smart phone, tablet, desktop and laptop environments with an internet browser. It is designed to work on most of today's devices but it can not be guaranteed to work in every device due to the variability.

3) You must only use latest versions of Safari, Chrome, Edge, Opera or Firefox browser. IE or other browsers aren't supported.

4) Although it supports landscape mode, it is recommended to use it in Portrait mode on mobile devices.

5) If you observe too large text and objects on Firefox/Mozilla browser, accessibility option might have been turned on. Disable it as below to see the scratcher in original size.


6) On Safari, if you see a blank page (Safari isn't loading websites or quits on your iPhone, iPad), please visit https://support.apple.com/en-us/102456 to troubleshoot. Among those most likely reason is wireless network. Try disabling the wireless network and switching to cellular network.

Error/Warning Messages:

1) "The font you chose doesnt fit to the screen. So please either choose different font or smaller font size." : This error is displayed when the font or font size for the title is too big to fit into the screen. Depending on the length of the title and font, this might happen. As the message says, either choose a different font, or choose "Smaller" or "Normal" font size. If you still get the message, try to use shorter words for the title.

2) "Your message under scratch area contains words that are too long to fit in. Please use shorter words or make sure you put space after punctuations. Please correct the error to continue." : This error is displayed when your message contains words that can't fit under scratch area and overflows. It should be fixed otherwise part of the message will not be visible. Use shorter words, shorter than 17 characters. See the example below. Arrows indicate whether a space is missing after punctuation or between two words.
