Scratch off Customization Guide



Customization for your personalized scratch off card is done using the customization panel. It is a panel that can be hidden to see the changes. Lets have a look at the elements of this panel before going into the instructions. Then The link for your personalized scratch off card can be created in 3 easy steps.


The panel's top bar can be used to hide the panel when clicked or touched.

Next is the Button for Help page. This is the button to show the help page. You can use "back" button of your browser to return to the customization page.

Button to hide the panel. This is the button to collapse the panel. There are 3 ways to hide the panel in order to see the page for changes you made. 1) click or touch to the panel's top bar, 2) click "Hide the Panel" button, 3) click or touch anywhere outside of the panel.

Panel's tabs for different parameters. There are 3 tabs to make changed on the page. "Step 1" tab is used for entering the custom title , the text under title and the message under the scratch area. The background image, font type and size of the title, shape of the scratch area and scratch area's foreground image can be changed on this tab.

Instructions:

1) Enter your family name above. It has max. 17 characters limit.

2) Your customized link will be automatically generated.

3) Click on your customized link to review. You can then click on the address bar of your browser and use share or copy option (or select all text while in the address bar and right click/long press to copy the address) to share the link with your family and friends. Make sure you dont add or remove characters after you paste the link while sharing.

Read the instructions pdf that comes with the purchase for more details.
